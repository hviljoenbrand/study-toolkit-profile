Study Toolkit Profile

A study-skills check-in

Companion to Study Tools for a New Generation

This is a check-in on how you study. Studying well is not one big skill, it is a handful of smaller ones that work together, and this looks at five of them. There are no right or wrong answers and nothing to pass or fail. All it asks is that you answer honestly, so we can show you what is already working well and where a little more attention would help most. These are habits, and habits can be built.

For each statement, choose the option that best matches how often it is true for you. When you submit, your profile appears on screen to read and keep.

Privacy notice

Who we are

The Study Toolkit Profile is provided by Study Toolbox. You can reach us at info@studytools.co.za or at www.studytools.co.za. For the purposes of South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Study Toolbox is the responsible party for your information.

What we collect

When you complete the profile, we collect your first name, surname, school, grade, the date you complete it, the answers you give, and a parent or guardian's email address. Your answers are used to work out your study profile, which includes a few indicators about wellbeing, such as sleep and managing stress.

Why we collect it

We use this information to create your study profile and show it to you on screen, to keep a copy so we can have a record and improve the assessment over time, and, where a counsellor or teacher is running the assessment with you, to share your profile with them. We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with any outside party for their own purposes.

Where it is kept

Your information is stored securely in the cloud, using a trusted service that holds it on our behalf and only on our instructions. That service may store it on servers outside South Africa, with appropriate safeguards in place. Only Study Toolbox can read it.

Who can see it

Only Study Toolbox, and, where relevant, the counsellor or teacher running the assessment with you. No one else.

How long we keep it

We keep your information for 12 months, and then delete it. You can ask us to delete it sooner.

Permission for under-18s

Most learners completing this are under 18. For that reason, a parent or guardian must give permission before the assessment is completed.

Your rights

You can ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it, and you can object to how we use it. Just email info@studytools.co.za. If you are not satisfied, you may also contact the Information Regulator of South Africa.

Marketing

We will not use the parent or guardian's email to send you marketing unless they have separately chosen to receive it.
